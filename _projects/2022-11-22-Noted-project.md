NOTED is a minimalistic, organized app where you can take down notes quickly. Whether you need to jot down a list of groceries, christmas checklist, things you need to do, notes for class, or whatever it may be, NOTED is the go to app. If you have any secrets that you want to hide and tell noone, NOTED has a special feature where you can store a secret note that is hidden within your regular notes just by the amount of taps you choose.
