LeftoverMate is your friendly food-saving companion that helps you track leftovers, avoid spoliage, make the most of your meals, reduce food waste, and stay organzied. Whether it's last night's dinner or a half forgotten meal sitting in the back of the fridge along with a few other leftovers you might also have forgotten about, LeftoverMate keeps everything in one place. Helps you organize with emojis, expiration dates, and helpful reminders. LeftoverMate can also be for groceries not just leftovers. It can even help track your leftover groceries or anything you have leftover in your pantry. Now available on the App Store, https://apps.apple.com/us/app/leftovermate/id6746516309
